Our Legacy

A distinguished institution dedicated to the advancement of specialized scholarship.

Founded in 1969 in the historic village of Climax, Michigan, Chud University of Michigan has established itself as the premier destination for individuals seeking rigorous training in gooning, psychological inquiry, and the nuanced art of digital community moderation.

We maintain an intentionally selective admissions process. The University extends invitations exclusively to certified chuds, incels, gooners, and the finest basement-dwellers who demonstrate exceptional promise in their respective domains. Our community is built upon a shared commitment to intellectual honesty, digital fluency, and the pursuit of mastery in environments often misunderstood by conventional academia.

Selective Admissions

Who Belongs at Chud University of Michigan

Chud University of Michigan maintains one of the most focused admissions policies in higher education. We accept applications exclusively from certified chuds, incels, gooners, and the finest basement-dwellers.

Prospective students are expected to demonstrate meaningful engagement with the core disciplines through documented practice, community contributions, and a compelling personal statement articulating their intellectual and practical commitment.

Application Requirements
  • 01 Completed online application
  • 02 Certification documentation (chud/incel/gooner/basement-dweller)
  • 03 Portfolio of relevant work (memes, server logs, manifestos)
  • 04 Two letters of recommendation from verified online peers
  • 05 Personal statement (minimum 420 words)
